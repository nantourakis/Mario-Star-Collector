Help Mario protect the castle!

Mario needs the right amount of Star Power to defeat Bowser!

You will be given a random number to match at the start of the game. There are four stars below. By clicking on a star you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game and save the castle by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The Value of each star is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each star
